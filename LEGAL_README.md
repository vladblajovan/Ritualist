## Support for These Documents

### Common Questions

**Q: Do I need a lawyer to review these?**
A: Highly recommended, especially before launch. A lawyer can ensure compliance with your specific situation and jurisdiction. Budget: $500-2,000 for a review.

**Q: Are these GDPR compliant?**
A: Yes. Section 10.2 of Privacy Policy covers all GDPR rights. Procedures in Setup Guide follow GDPR timelines (30 days).

**Q: Are these CCPA compliant?**
A: Yes. Section 10.3 of Privacy Policy covers all CCPA rights. Procedures in Setup Guide follow CCPA timelines (45 days).

**Q: Can I just copy Apple's legal documents?**
A: No. Apple's documents don't address your specific features. These documents are customized for Ritualist's architecture (serverless, iCloud, geofencing, etc.).

**Q: What if I add new features later?**
A: Update your Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. See Maintenance section in LEGAL_SUMMARY.md.

**Q: Can I charge users for data requests?**
A: No. GDPR and CCPA both require free responses to user rights requests.

**Q: How long do I keep compliance records?**
A: Minimum 3 years. See LEGAL_SETUP_GUIDE.md Section 5.4.
