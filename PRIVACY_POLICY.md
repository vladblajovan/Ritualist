# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Ritualist ("App," "we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

Ritualist is developed by Vlad Blajovan, an individual developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand our practices regarding your personal data.

**Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.** If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Habit Data**
- Habit names, descriptions, and details you create
- Completion logs and timestamps
- Streak information and habit history
- Notes and reflections you add to habits

**Location Data (Optional)**
- Your precise location coordinates when you enable geofencing for location-based reminders
- Location data is used locally on your device and synced via iCloud CloudKit
- Location information is only collected when you explicitly enable the geofencing feature

**Account Information**
- Apple ID (used solely for iCloud CloudKit synchronization)
- No separate account creation required; authentication is managed entirely through Apple's ecosystem

**Payment Information**
- In-app purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store
- We do not directly collect, store, or process payment card information
- Subscription and purchase data is managed by Apple's billing systems

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device and Usage Information**
- Device model and operating system version
- App crashes and performance data (through Apple's built-in crash reporting)
- Approximate app usage patterns (not individual habit details)
- iOS system logs related to the App

**iCloud Sync Data**
- Your habit data is automatically synced to Apple CloudKit when you sign in with your Apple ID
- CloudKit is Apple's secure cloud database; we do not host this data
- See Apple's privacy policy for CloudKit at apple.com/privacy

**Analytics**
- We use Apple's built-in App Analytics (via App Store Connect) to understand app performance and user trends
- No third-party analytics services (Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.) are integrated
- Analytics data is aggregated and does not personally identify you

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

**On-Device Storage**
- Your habit data and personality analysis are primarily stored on your local device
- This data is encrypted by iOS's native security features

**iCloud CloudKit**
- When you sign in with your Apple ID, your data syncs to Apple's CloudKit infrastructure
- CloudKit servers are located in multiple geographic regions; see apple.com/privacy for details
- Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest through Apple's security protocols

### 4.2 Data Retention

**Active Account**
- While you use the App, your habit data is retained in full

**After Account Deactivation or App Deletion**
- When you delete the App from your device, local data is deleted
- iCloud data may persist in your iCloud account; you can manage this through Apple's iCloud settings
- You can request permanent deletion of your data by contacting us (see Section 11)
- We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of your request

## 7. Location and Geofencing

### 7.1 Location Data Collection

The geofencing feature:
- Requires your explicit permission via iOS location permissions
- Uses iOS CLLocationManager to detect when you enter or exit defined geographic areas
- Triggers local push notifications based on your geofence settings
- Does NOT share location coordinates with our servers
- Location data used for geofencing is stored locally and synced via iCloud CloudKit

### 7.2 Your Control

- You control location access through iOS Settings > Privacy > Location Services
- You can revoke location permission at any time
- Geofencing will not function if location access is denied
- Disabling location tracking does not affect other App features

### 7.3 Privacy Protection

- Location data is never shared with third parties
- Location coordinates are not used for analytics or tracking
- Geofencing operates only on your device; no location data is sent to our servers

## 13. Security

### 13.1 How We Protect Your Data

We implement multiple layers of security:

**On-Device Encryption**
- iOS encrypts your local habit data at rest
- Device encryption requires your device passcode

**iCloud Encryption**
- iCloud CloudKit encrypts data in transit and at rest
- Apple manages encryption keys; see apple.com/privacy for details

**No Unencrypted Transmission**
- All data transmitted between your device and iCloud uses HTTPS/TLS encryption

**Access Controls**
- Only your Apple ID can access your iCloud data
- We don't maintain server-side access to user data

### 13.2 Security Limitations

While we implement industry-standard security practices, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against:
- Unauthorized access through compromised device credentials
- Physical device theft
- Exploitation of operating system vulnerabilities
- Intentional malicious attacks

## Appendix B: Privacy Settings Guide

### How to Adjust Privacy Settings in Ritualist

**Disable Geofencing**
1. Open Ritualist > Settings > Location
2. Toggle "Enable Geofencing" to Off
3. Confirm in iOS Settings > Privacy > Location Services

**Disable Notifications**
1. Open Ritualist > Settings > Notifications
2. Toggle notification types to Off
3. Or manage through iOS Settings > Notifications > Ritualist

**Disable Personality Analysis**
1. Open Ritualist > Settings > Privacy
2. Toggle "Personality Analysis" to Off

**Export Your Data**
1. Open Ritualist > Settings > Data & Privacy
2. Tap "Export Data"
3. Your data will be downloaded as JSON

**Delete Your Account Data**
1. Contact privacy@ritualist.app with your request
2. Verify your identity
3. Your data will be deleted within 30 days
