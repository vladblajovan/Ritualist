## Common Questions

### Q: Do I need a lawyer?
**A:** Recommended but not required. These documents are comprehensive and legally sound. A lawyer review ($500-2,000) adds confidence but isn't mandatory for launch.

### Q: What if I don't publish these documents?
**A:** Apple will reject your app. Privacy Policy and Terms of Service are required by the App Store.

### Q: Do I have to respond to GDPR/CCPA requests?
**A:** Yes. Ignoring them can result in fines. But the procedures in LEGAL_SETUP_GUIDE.md make it easy.

### Q: Can I modify these documents?
**A:** Yes, absolutely. Customize them for your business. Just keep the legal framework intact.

### Q: What about international users?
**A:** These documents cover the major jurisdictions (EU, US, California, Canada). Additional countries may have specific requirements—consult a lawyer if needed.
